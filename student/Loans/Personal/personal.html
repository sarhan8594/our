Federal Bank Loans

Apply for instant Federal Bank personal loans up to Rs. 25 Lakhs online at attractive rates of interest starting from 11.49% p.a. for tenure up to 48 months. The processing fee is up to 3% of the loan amount and prepayment can be done at Nil charges for floating rate schemes.

  • Debit/Credit Cards: Federal Bank offers special debit/credit cards for students with features such as cashback, discounts, and other offers.
  • Loan Amount: The loan amount varies depending on the course and the institute you have applied for. Typically, banks offer loans up to Rs. 10 lakhs for studies in India and up to Rs. 20 lakhs for studies abroad. However, some banks may offer higher amounts based on the applicant's profile and the course selected.
  • Interest Rate: The interest rate on student loans is usually lower than the regular personal loans. It ranges between 8.50% to 12.50%, depending on the bank, the loan amount, and the repayment period.
  • Repayment: The repayment period for student loans is usually between 5 to 7 years, but it may vary depending on the bank's policies. Some banks may also offer a moratorium period where you don't have to pay the loan EMI until the course completion or a few months after getting a job.
  • Interest Calculation: The interest on a student loan is calculated based on the outstanding loan amount and the interest rate. The interest is typically calculated on a daily or monthly reducing balance, which means that the interest is charged only on the outstanding principal amount and not on the entire loan amount.
